Who am I?

I have been around computers my whole life and I like to type words on a computer. On a more serious note, I always knew I was going to work with technology and computers when I grew up. My inspiration to becoming a software engineer specifically started during my final undergraduate semester. I had to build an app from scratch with a team of 3 other members. Despite not having the technical skills to build a fully functional app, we were still able to put together a presentable product along with a couple features. I was proud of the work my team and I produced, but it did not stop there. I wanted to create something bigger and better. For the next few months after graduating undergrad, I spent studying programming from scratch. It was going well initially, but I ran into a wall eventually, not knowing whether I was heading down the right path, or whether what I was studying was even relevant anymore. After graduating from App Academy's Full-Stack Software Engineering Bootcamp in May 2022, I am proud to start my journey as a Software Engineer.
